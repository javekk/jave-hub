Who is jave?

In my life I always wanted to open a blog, share my thoughts and feelings with unknown guys across the whole world with no specific reasons, but I also thought I had nothing interesting or funny to share, I am not good in anything and the problem was: what should I write in there?

Then I realised I don't really care about what to write, I just wanted to create the blog from scratch, put together some HTML and JavaScript and enjoy implementing a "web site". Yea, it seems sad, maybe it does, but meh, people have fun in different ways, this is one of those ways in which I have fun. So, who jave is, it is not a problem, this is just a blog in which I put some of the things I do in my life, espicially code, but also stories, reflections, feelings, etc..
